Restrictions

  • NO Smoking is allowed inside any of the buildings on the property of The Barn at Dunvilla, including the deck, the patio, the log cabin, and The Boathouse. There is a designated smoking area outside on the front lawn more than 30’ away from The Barn.
  • NO open flames are allowed on the property.
  • Fire Code prohibits any candles, fireworks or open flames inside the buildings. Battery operated candles are allowed and available from The Barn.

We have a “Leave no Trace” policy. Any items used must be returned to their original location.

Capacity

The Barn accommodates 280 guests. A final head count is required three weeks before event, so that appropriate staffing and linens may be scheduled.

Music/Band/DJ

The band or DJ may setup in the loft in The Barn. Appropriate electrical outlets, seating, tables and lighting are provided. The band or DJ will have the last dance at 11:30 pm to allow for time to tear down prior to the midnight deadline. The county regulates that we keep sound to a decibel level that is friendly to our neighbors and our staff will monitor this for events.

Our Wedding Season

We host weddings and events throughout the year. Contact us to see which weekends are available.

On-Site Assistance

Our staff will be onsite for the day of your event. Our staff will assist with monitoring the facility, freshening up restrooms and paper products, answering questions, and helping to facilitate the day. Our staff will have the chairs and tables set for the start of your event. Any changing of tables and chairs during the event is the responsibility of the Client.

Ceremony Sites

The Barn has three beautiful ceremony sites including the Loft, the Oak Tree, and the Pond. These are available for year-round events, weather permitting.

Vendor Setup & Delivery

Clients and vendors must load-in and load-out on the setup day or the day of the event.

Catering

The Barn appreciates that individual tastes lead to all types of catering and cuisine. With this in mind, we welcome our Clients to invite the caterer of their choice to serve for their event. If you would like recommendations for some quality caterers, we will be happy to share our highly recommended vendors with you.

All caterers onsite must be Board of Health (BOH) approved and have proof of liability insurance. The caterer is to provide full cooking and serving staff. The Barn has a food prep area for caterers including 220 outlets, stainless counters, stainless sinks, stainless prep tables, and outdoor space with electricity and water. The catering company is responsible for the setup, breakdown and cleanup of the kitchen and catering area.

If you choose to cater the food yourselves, you are required to obtain event insurance policy to cover all food served that day. A copy of the policy must be provided to The Barn at Dunvilla at least 30 days prior to the event. If a policy is not provided, food will not be allowed on site.

The Barn at Dunvilla reserves the right to approve or deny any caterer. Any caterer who has not met our standards and who has not followed these regulations may not be approved and denied access to the facility.

Service of Alcoholic Beverages

Our bar is hosted exclusively by Lakeside Tavern from Detroit Lakes. They specialize in working with our Clients to provide exceptional quality, efficiency and service. You will work with them choosing the bar menu of your choice for your contract with them.

  • Outside alcohol is not allowed to be brought into the venue, parking lot or surrounding grounds.
  • All alcohol must be served by Lakeside Tavern.
  • Swizzle sticks are prohibited.
  • No bar can be left unattended at any time. No guests behind the bar at any time. There must be at least one (1) bartender throughout the night until the bar closes. No Exceptions!
  • The bar will be closed and alcohol service stopped once the bartender leaves.
  • Catering wait-staff will serve alcoholic beverages at an open bar as described in your event contract and during the meal to non-intoxicated persons of legal drinking age.
  • The final decisions whether any particular guest may continue to be served alchohol rests with the on-site bar catering staff. Clients will publicly support any decision made by management and catering to refuse to serve an intoxicated or under age guest an alcoholic beverage.
  • The caterer will take all reasonable steps to see that guests do not serve alcoholic beverages to themselves, or each other. The Barn at Dunvilla LLC, its agents, officers, directors, employees, or otherwise maintains the right to refuse service to any guest at any time.

Decorations

We recommend that you assign someone to take home all items brought into The Barn (i.e., decorations) at the end of your event. All items not belonging to The Barn need to be removed by the end of the event.

  • No nails, tacks or staples may be used inside the barn.
  • Rice or birdseed may be allowed outdoors with prior permission. Confetti and balloons are NOT allowed.

Parking

The designated parking area is on the east side of The Barn at Dunvilla and will be marked with signage. Weather conditions may be a little wet after heavy rains on a country estate and all drivers and busses (if applicable) must stay on the gravel surface indicated, or in designated grass parking areas. Any damage to the property from the bus companies or private vehicles will be repaired and paid for by them, or by the wedding party.

Alcohol and driving represent increased risks that are the full responsibility of the wedding party and their guests. All driving and parking on The Barn at Dunvilla is at guest’s own risk.

Damage/Security Deposit/Rental Fee Deposit

The Barn at Dunvilla may require a $500 refundable damage deposit. If damage to the venue or property occurs, the Client agrees to pay for any damage and/or cleaning charges for the function space that may occur during the client contract period. The Client will not be responsible, however, for ordinary wear and tear or for damage that was caused by persons other than the Client and attendees. This includes additional cleaning not completed to the standards outlined in the Caterer/Partner Agreement.

Rental Deposit

The deposit is non-refundable. The balance of the full rental fee is due 60 days before the event. If payment is not received at that time, your event will be cancelled and all monies will be forfeited.
